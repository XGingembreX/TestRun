Xpert Peel

Ingredients

  • Salicylic acid
  • Glycolic acid
  • Lactic acid
  • Phytic acid
  • Azelaic acid

Benefits

  • Fresher, tighter, smoother, firmer skin
  • No downtime peel
  • Immediate glowing skin
  • The 5-acid blend treats pigmentation, anti-aging, acne, and dehydration
  • It is safe for Fitzpatrick 1 to 6

Indications

Acne, photo-damage, pigmentation, scarring, blemishes.

Contra-indications

  • Roaccutane users
  • Diabetics
  • Skin disease and disorders (eczema, psoriasis)
  • Pregnancy
  • Sunburn

Precare, postcare and expectations

  • Stop use of Retinol, Benzoyl Peroxide, Vit C, AHA's, BHA's 3-5 days pre and post peel.
  • 3-5 days pre and post peel no dermaplaning or waxing.
  • 7 days prior and post peel no laser.
  • No granular of enzyme exfoliator in between 2 weekly treatments.
  • Avoid sun exposure.
