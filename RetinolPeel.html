Retinol Peel

Ingredients

  • Retinol 3% (Vitamin A)
  • Bisabolol
  • Vitamin E
  • Botanical extract Helichrysum

Benefits

  • Effective in lifting pigmentation
  • Skin looks flawless
  • Amazing glow
  • Suites for all Flitzpatrick skintypes
  • The peel is painless
  • Assitance in the production of collagen and elastin
  • Improves skin tone and skin texture
  • Controls oil production
  • Exfoliates

Indications

Fine lines and wrinkles, enlarged pores, scaring, acne, hyperpigmentation and anti-aging.

Contra-indications

  • Active dermatitis (eczema, psoriasis and roscacea)
  • Herpes Simplex
  • Cancer
  • Diabetes
  • Pregnancy or breastfeeding
  • Viatmin A sensitivity
  • Cortisone, antibiotics or Roaccutan
  • Autoimmune disorders
  • Skin hypersensitivity
  • Sunburn

Precare, postcare and expectations

  • 7 to 10 days of downtime, the peeling process may vary for each client.
  • Clients who have dehydrated skin will have more visible peel.
  • In the first 3 days, the clients will have the most visible peel.
  • Keep skin dry.
  • Do not pick/scratch skin.
  • Advisable to go straight home post peel.
  • Leave peel on from 4 to 8 hours. Wash of peel at home with water.
  • In addition with Microneedling: leave on for 24 hours.
  • Frequency: every 4 to 6 weeks.
  • No exfoiating products like Retinol, AHAs and benzoyl peroxide to be used 5 to 10 days before and after the Retinol peel.
  • No exfoliating treatments or aesthetic treatments for 2 weeks before and after peel.
  • Avoid sun exposure.
  • 3 to 4 peels recommended 6 weekly.
  • Redness, peeling, tighening, itching, burning sensation is normal.
