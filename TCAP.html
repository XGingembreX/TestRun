TCA Peel

Ingredients

Trichloroacetic acid

Benefits

  • Smoothes wrinkles and lines
  • Improves skin texture
  • Refines pores
  • Clarifies skin
  • Remove dead skin cells
  • Stimulates new cell growth
  • Stimulates collagen production
  • Reduce appearance of acne scars
  • A series of 2 to 6 peels can be done every 6 to 8 weeks

Indications

Fine lines, wrinkles, rough uneven skin, acne scars, hyperpigmentation and sun damage.

Contra-indications

  • Excessive sun exposure and sunburn
  • Cold sores
  • Keloid scarring
  • Accutane user
  • Pregnancy and lactation
  • Radioactive or chemotherapy treatments

Precare, postcare and expectations

  • Avoid sun exposure for 2 weeks pre and post peel.
  • Avoid heat and excerise that will cause excessive sweating and perspiring during the first several days after the peel.
  • Discontinue exfoliators 10 days prior to peel and discontinue retinol 7 days prior to peel and 4 weeks post peel.
  • Only cleanse skin 24 hours post peel.
  • Swelling, puffiness, redness, peeling, flaking, stinging, itching, burning, mild pain, tightness is possible.
  • Acne flare-up post peel is possible.
  • Downtime is 3 to 10 days.
